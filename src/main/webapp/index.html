What is ChemVantage?

ChemVantage is an open educational resource that provides high‑quality question banks and auto‑graded assessments for introductory general chemistry courses.

  • Large, diverse item banks with randomized parameters
  • Automatic scoring with grade passback to your LMS
  • Designed for accessibility and equitable learning

For Students

Do your chemistry homework, quizzes, and practice directly inside your course LMS. No separate account needed.

Privacy

ChemVantage uses LTI to receive user and course information from your LMS for the purposes of authentication, delivering assessments, and returning grades. Data are retained only as long as necessary to support these functions.

  • Personal data shared via LTI depends on your LMS configuration and role.
  • Data are not shared with or sold to third parties.
  • No user Personal Identifiable Information (PII) is stored on ChemVantage servers
  • Instructors and administrators can request data removal as permitted by institutional policy.

See the detailed ChemVantage privacy policy.

Accessibility

This site is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 AA. We use semantic HTML, descriptive link text, labels for form fields, keyboard‑accessible controls, and high‑contrast colors.

  • Navigate by keyboard using Tab/Shift+Tab; focus states are always visible.
  • Images and icons include suitable alternative text or ARIA labels.
  • Content reflows for small screens without loss of information or functionality.

Please report any accessibility barriers to admin@chemvantage.org.
