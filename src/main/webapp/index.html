What is ChemVantage?

ChemVantage is an open educational resource that provides high‑quality question banks and auto‑graded assessments for introductory general chemistry courses.

  • Large, diverse item banks with randomized parameters
  • Automatic scoring with grade passback to your LMS
  • Designed for accessibility and equitable learning

For Students

Do your chemistry homework, quizzes, and practice directly inside your course LMS. No separate account needed.

How you access

  • Open your LMS course
  • Select the ChemVantage assignment
  • Launch and submit your work

What you get

  • Instant feedback and scoring
  • Multiple attempts when enabled
  • Practice aligned to your course

If you don’t see ChemVantage in your course, ask your instructor how to get access.

Add ChemVantage to your course

  1. Ask your LMS administrator to enable the ChemVantage LTI 1.3 tool for your institution.
  2. In your course, create an assignment in ChemVantage as an External Tool using Deep Linking.
  3. Select the desired assignment type and publish.
  4. When the assignment launches, set policies and select question items from the Instructor Page.
ChemVantage supports LTI 1.3 Advantage services: Deep Linking, Assignment and Grade Services, and Names and Role Provisioning.

For LMS Administrators

Configure ChemVantage as a trusted LTI 1.3 Advantage tool for secure, standards‑based interoperability.

Privacy

ChemVantage uses LTI to receive user and course information from your LMS for the purposes of authentication, delivering assessments, and returning grades. Data is retained only as long as necessary to support these functions.

  • Personal data shared via LTI depends on your LMS configuration and role.
  • Data is not shared with or sold to third parties.
  • No user Personal Identifiable Information (PII) is stored on ChemVantage servers
  • Instructors and administrators can request data removal as permitted by institutional policy.

See the detailed ChemVantage privacy policy.

Accessibility

This site is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 AA. We use semantic HTML, descriptive link text, labels for form fields, keyboard‑accessible controls, and high‑contrast colors.

  • Navigate by keyboard using Tab/Shift+Tab; focus states are always visible.
  • Images and icons include suitable alternative text or ARIA labels.
  • Content reflows for small screens without loss of information or functionality.

Report any accessibility barriers to admin@chemvantage.org.
