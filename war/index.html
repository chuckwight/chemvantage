ChemVantage
 An Open Education Resource for General Chemistry


Let's face it. Chemistry is hard.

But with commitment and lots of practice, almost anyone can master it. ChemVantage was founded in 2010 on the idea that the most effective learning takes place during repeated intermittent engagement with the subject material. By offering several kinds of online assignments, we help you challenge students to
  • prepare themselves in advance for class discussions
  • engage with in-class polls using their own phones or laptops (no clickers required)
  • apply their new knowledge to solving computer-graded homework problems and get immediate feedback
  • work in teams to promote collaborative learning and peer instruction
... all at about 1/10 the cost of our nearest competitor.

ChemVantage is proud to partner with OpenStax, the largest provider of OER textbooks for General Chemistry. We provide tools that are aligned with the OpenStax Chemistry/2e and Chemistry: Atoms First/2e texts, including a new SmartText app that combines reading assignments with adaptive questions covering key concepts in each chapter. This ensures that your students are reading and understanding the content in a timely way.

ChemVantage works with your learning management system using LTI

You create ChemVantage assignments in your LMS, and the scores are posted automatically to the LMS grade book. No student personal identifiable information (PII) is stored on ChemVantage servers, so your data are always secure.

Start with a placement exam

Getting students into the right course at the right time is essential. The ChemVantage placement exam assesses
  • knowledge of basic chemistry
  • essential math skills
  • ability to interpret word problems
so the right mitigation steps can be taken to ensure student success.

Then create ChemVantage assignments for your students

With a few clicks in your LMS, you can assign students to watch instructional videos with embedded questions, take short quizzes on the reading assignment, use in-class polls, work homework problems, and complete practice exams to validate their learning. ChemVantage makes it easy.

ChemVantage is designed to be very low cost

Our open source software is deployed on Google App Engine servers, which provide massive scaling at extremely low cost. The services are heavily text-based with no fancy widgets. We own all our content, so we don't pay royalties to publishers. We pass these savings on to our users, and for many students and institutions, ChemVantage is completely free.
